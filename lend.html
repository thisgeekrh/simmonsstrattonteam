Meet Our Preferred Lender




Joe Campo, NBI Financial Group


I am an experienced mortgage professional with a background in both residential and commercial lending. I began my career as a traditional loan officer. After a year and a half, I transitioned to a specialized sales group for the 10th largest mortgage lender in the country. As the top performer for 5 months, I was then tasked with leading the team. Over the next 3.5 years, I grew the team to over 100 employees, generating billions of dollars in monthly funding volume and becoming the 2nd largest lender nationwide.
In April of 2022, the lender was sold, prompting me to return to the basics. Instead of joining the acquiring lender, I seized the opportunity to join NBI Financial Group as an equity partner. Founded by two industry-leading mortgage professionals, Nick and Ben, NBI focuses on options and expedited closings. We are partnered with over 40 top mortgage lenders, enabling us to offer competitive rates and find the right product for each client.
My goal is to provide options and education to every client, ensuring they are comfortable with their payments and positioned for financial freedom. We also educate clients on additional revenue streams through commercial lending. Whether you're a first-time homebuyer or seasoned investor, I am committed to delivering unmatched service.
